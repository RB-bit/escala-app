Privacy Policy - ESCALA

Last updated: March 6, 2026

ESCALA is a local development tool designed for e-commerce entrepreneurs to manage and optimize their Meta Ads campaigns. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

ESCALA operates entirely in your browser as a local development application. We do NOT store, process, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processing happens locally on your machine.

  • Meta Ads Data: Campaign performance, ad creatives, and account information are fetched directly from Meta's API and displayed locally.
  • Tienda Nube Data: Store orders and product information are retrieved via API and shown only in your browser session.
  • Google Drive: File listings and assets are accessed through Google's OAuth and never stored by ESCALA.

2. How We Use Your Information

All integrations (Meta Ads, Tienda Nube, Google Drive) are used solely to:

  • Display real-time campaign performance dashboards
  • Enable creation and management of ad campaigns
  • Facilitate asset selection from your cloud storage
  • Provide AI-powered recommendations via Claude API

3. API Permissions

ESCALA requests the following Meta permissions exclusively for ad management functionality:

  • ads_management - Create and manage ad campaigns
  • ads_read - Read campaign performance data
  • pages_manage_ads - Publish ads on behalf of Facebook Pages
  • pages_read_engagement - Access page insights
  • business_management - Manage business assets

These permissions are required by Meta's API to perform ad operations and are not used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

ESCALA integrates with:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. - For ads management (subject to Meta's Data Policy)
  • Tienda Nube - For e-commerce data (subject to Tienda Nube's Privacy Policy)
  • Google LLC - For Drive file access (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic PBC - For AI coaching features (Claude API calls made directly from browser)

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Security

Since ESCALA runs locally:

  • No database or backend server stores your information
  • API tokens are stored in local environment files (.env.local) and never transmitted
  • All sessions are browser-based and cleared when you close the tab

6. Your Rights

As all data remains on your device, you have complete control. You can:

  • Revoke API access at any time through Meta Business Suite, Tienda Nube, or Google Account settings
  • Delete local environment files to remove all stored tokens
  • Clear browser storage to erase session data

7. Children's Privacy

ESCALA is designed for business use and is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. For significant changes, we will notify users through the application interface.
